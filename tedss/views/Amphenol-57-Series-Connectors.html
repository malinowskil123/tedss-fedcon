Amphenol 57 Series Connectors

HOME > RESOURCES > Amphenol 57 Series Connectors

Specs

The Amphenol 57 Series of miniature ribbon connectors are a reliable, versatile, and cost effective method of meeting multi-wire cable terminations.

Common configuration types include

  • Rack to Panel
  • Cable to Panel
  • Cable to Cable

Common Connector Styles:

  • Series 57–micro-ribbons-solder
  • Series 157-micro-pierce-solderless
  • Series 57LE-micro-ribbon-all plastic right angle

Contact configurations include

  • 14
  • 24
  • 36
  • 50

Mating surfaces with flexing action from both contacts, form tight connection for electrical continuity. Positive mating action wipes the contacts clean of foreign substances for low contact resistance. Zinc plated outer shell with chromate finish for corrosion protection. Solder cup contact tails for wire connection and pin style tails for PCB attachment. 157 series offer solderless termination and the same reliability and features of the 57 series. 57LE an all plastic connector that uses metal front shells to obtain EMI/RFI shielding capability. Right angle header allows mass termination from PCB to equipment panels.

57-40240–24 contact receptacle mating connection with spring latches

157-32240 24 contact cable mount plug

57LE-20240-7700(D35G) 24 contact right angle PCB terminal socket
