Bussmann Puts Hold On Shipping Select Military Fuses and Fuse Holders

Due to a DLA issue with a few select military fuses and fuse holders, Bussmann will stop shipping all of the part numbers listed in the letter notification (click on PDF icon to download) they provided. This will be in effect until further notice.

Note: all part numbers on the list marked in orange will become obsolete and no longer produced.
