Military FuseHolders : MIL-F-19207

A fuse holder is a device designed for protecting and mounting fuses. Fuse holders are available in two different types open and fully enclosed. Military grade fuseholders are fully enclosed in order to be shock proof and resistant to extreme temperatures and weather conditions. Military fuseholders are compatible with both commercial and military (MIL-F-15160) fuses however they are designed for use with military fuses. You can identify a military fuseholder by the color of its body which typically is gray. To get more information on a specific part number please use our Part Number App below!

DoD* Standards for MIL-PRF-19207G

  • Salt Atmosphere (Corrosion)
  • Barometric Pressure (Reduced)
  • Moisture Resistance.
  • Thermal Shock Resistance
  • High Impact and Shock Resistance
  • Insulation Resistance
  • Contact Resistance
